OUTCOMIST - O-Integrated Concepts

Using the "O" as the symbolic anchor, like Alchemist's "A"

CONCEPT A: TARGET O (CONVERGING)

Symbolism

Target/Convergence: All paths lead to the outcome. The arrows converging on the O represent how Outcomist guides everything toward clarity and action. The rings inside show progressive focus.

CONCEPT B: RIPPLE O (RADIATING)

Symbolism

Progressive Discovery: The ripples expanding outward represent Tier 1 → 2 → 3 → 4. Start at the center (quick insight), expand outward as needed (deeper analysis). Discovery radiates from the core question.

CONCEPT C: LENS O (CLARITY)

Symbolism

Clarity Through Focus: The O as a lens that brings scattered thoughts into sharp focus. Light rays converge through the lens, representing how Outcomist clarifies confused decisions into clear direction.

CONCEPT D: CROSSHAIR O (PRECISION)

Symbolism

Precision Targeting: The crosshair represents finding the exact right answer. Outcomist helps you zero in on what matters, cutting through noise to hit the target with precision.
