Discovery Concepts - Not Lame

"The answer was here all along, you just couldn't see it"

1. The Aperture (Eye Opening)

Meaning: Camera aperture opening wider to let light in and reveal the image. The O opens up to show you what was always there but hidden in darkness. Professional, precise, not corny.

2. The Keyhole (Unlocking Insight)

Meaning: The O is a locked door. The keyhole reveals light streaming through - the insight you unlock. You found the key (the right question), now you can see what was locked away.

3. The Excavation (Digging to Truth)

Meaning: Layers being stripped away (excavated) to reveal what's buried underneath. Archeological - you dig through surface soil to find the artifact. Professional, suggests depth and discovery.

4. The Spotlight (Illuminating Hidden Truth)

Meaning: Spotlight beam illuminating what was hidden in darkness. The O is the stage, the beam reveals what's at center stage. Clean, professional, suggests clarity and focus.

5. The Reveal (Curtain Pulled Back)

Meaning: Curtain pulled aside to reveal what was behind it. Left side still covered (the old understanding), right side revealed (the insight). Theatrical but sophisticated.
