Why Manus-Inspired Design Is Best

  • Much larger input area — Textarea (not single-line input) invites detailed descriptions
  • Prominent question as heading — "What decision are you facing?" is personal and direct
  • No visual clutter — Removed logo, accent line, tagline - just the essentials
  • Subtle example discovery — "Explore example decisions" link at bottom, not in your face
  • Premium spacing — Generous breathing room makes it feel high-quality
  • Larger input = more inviting — Signals "take your time, be detailed"
  • Better first impression — Immediately actionable, not marketing-heavy
